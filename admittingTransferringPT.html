Tasks in

Manage Patient

Tasks in 'Manage Patient' include transfering patients to other rooms, discharging patients, clearing sectors, putting the monitor on standby, adding groups & confirming paced modes.


Admit Patient:


First hover over the patient sector, the action buttons will appear. Select Manage Patient. A new panel will pop up. Select 'Find Patient'.




Next you need to type the patients MRN into the search box labeled MRN. Once you hit search the patients information will auto populate. The only information that needs to be added is the patient age. There age should be added under the section labeled 'Screen Notes'. Lastly confirm paced mode, select 'ON' if the patient has a pacemaker, otherwise select 'OFF'.


Transfer Patient:


To transfer a patient howver over the patient sector. The action buttons will appear, select 'Manage Patient' Navigate to the right side of the panel, under the buttons labeled permanent is a transfer button. Select transfer. Now select the unit & room the patient has transfered to.


Groups



At Nortons we typically only select groups for patients based on tele orders. If the patient has WOI orders then select w/o. This will put a orange box around the patients name. If they have orders to be followed by cardiology then select ULP. This will place a blue box around there name.


Pair Devices to Monitor:




To pair a device such as an X2, X3 or a tele-pack start by hovering over the patient sector. The action buttons will appear. Next select 'Manage Patient'. Next select Equipment. A list of avaliable equipment will appear to the left. Use the arrows to remove or add a device.

If the device you are trying to pair is not listed then that usually means it is 'paired' to another patient already. If you hover over the room numbers in each patient sector the paired devices will display making it quick & easy to see what device is paired to each room.


Transport/Standby




To place a monitor on standby hover over the patient sector until the action buttons appear. Select 'manage patient'. Next Select the button on the right labeled 'Transport/Standby'.

Another panel will pop up prompting you to make a selection. You must check the equipment box for the monitor to standby.


Clearing Sectors



Start by hovering over the patient sector. The action buttons will appear, select 'Manage Patient'. On the far right, select the last button labeled 'Clear Sector'in the 'permanent' stack. A pop-up will appear asking if you wish to stop the overview of the patient sector you selected. To clear the sector select 'yes'.

What is the difference between discharging a patient and clearing a sector? Discharging a patient discharges the patient information & recorded data from the bedside monitor. Clearing a sector stops the overview of a sector, but does not discharge patient information & data.
