Nurses Guide

This section is meant as a guide, for nurses pulled to the monitor-tech role. The responsibilities for nurses that are pulled into this role are different from that of the monitor-tech.

As a nurse filling the monitor-tech role you are required to save strips at specific times depending upon which shift you are working. During the day 1400 strips are saved, and at night 2200 & 0600 strips are saved.

Along with saving strips you also must monitor and alert nurses to changes in patient rhythms.
