Select & Set Parameters:




Left click on or beside heart rate or Spo2 numeric, and a small box will pop up. In this box you can set the heart rate limits.


Change leads/waves & adjust Size.




Left click beside the waveform that you want to change. ***NOTE: If the patient has an A-line then it should be set as the secondary waveform.

Left click on the lead numeric and select lead. In this panel you can also size up or size down the waveform.

Alarm Messages:



If a patient is yellow or red alarming click on the alarm message in the top right corner of the patient sector to view what triggered the alarm.

***Warning: If you click anywhere in the patient sector it will silence the alarm. Per policy alarms should not be silenced from the monitor room this could cause a delay in life-saving intervention and lead to an unfavorable patient outcome.
